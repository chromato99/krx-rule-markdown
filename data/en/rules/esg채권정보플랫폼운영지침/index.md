CHAPTER I. GENERAL PROVISIONS 

§1. Purpose 

The  purpose  of  these  Guidelines  is  to  define  the  general  requirements  for  the  Korea 
Exchange to operate a segment dedicated to Socially responsible investment bonds. 

§2. Definitions 
(Amended on September 20, 2022) 

The terms used in these Guidelines shall be defined as follows: 

1.  The  term  “socially  responsible  investment  bond”  means  green  bonds,  social  bonds, 
sustainability bonds, and sustainability-linked bonds. 

2.  The  term  “green  bond”  means  debt  securities  (including  debt-security  beneficiary 
certificates pursuant to [§111] of the KOSPI Market Listing Regulation; the same hereinafter) 
issued  to  raise  funds  for  investment  in  environmentally  friendly  projects  or  social 
infrastructure. 

3. The term “social bond” means debt securities issued to raise funds to invest in social value 
creation business. 

4. The term “sustainability bond” means debt securities issued to raise funds for investment 
in businesses that are environmentally friendly and create social value. 

5. The term “sustainability-linked bonds” means debt securities of which the financial and/or 
structural  characteristics  can  vary  depending  on  whether  the  predefined  sustainability 
performance objectives are achieved. 

6. The term “use-of-proceeds bond” means debt securities falling under any of Subparagraphs 
2 through 4. 

7.  The  term  “socially  responsible  investment  bond-dedicated  segment”  means  the  Internet 
homepage  and  similar  information  media  (hereinafter  referred  to  as  “dedicated  segment”) 
operated  by  the  Korea  Exchange  (hereinafter  referred  to  as  the  “Exchange”)  to  provide 
information on socially responsible investment bond. 

8.  The  term  “principles  of  socially  responsible  investment  bond,  etc.”  means  principles, 
standards,  guidelines,  etc.  established  by  the  state,  international  organizations,  or  reputable 
private  organization  (referring  to  the  International  Capital  Market  Association  and  the 
Climate Bonds Initiative, etc.) in relation to Socially responsible investment bond. 

9. The term “socially responsible investment bond framework” means a document in which 
an  issuer  describes  core  components  specified  in  the  socially  responsible  investment  bond 

1 

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
principles  and  other  necessary  matters  in  order  to  issue  and  manage  socially  responsible 
investment bonds. 

10.  The  term  “external  reviewers”  means  those  with  expertise  that  meet  requirements 
pursuant  to  the  Guidelines  for  Green,  Social,  Sustainability,  and  Sustainability-Linked 
Bonds  External  Reviews  of  the  International  Capital  Market  Association  or  the  Climate 
Bonds Standard of the Climate Bonds Initiative. 

§3.  Bonds  for  registration 

Debt securities eligible for the registration in the dedicated segment shall be those for which 
applications are submitted by corporations that listed the relevant debt securities. (Amended 
on September 20, 2022) 

§4. Registration requirements   

(1)  Debt  securities  applying  for  the  registration  in  the  dedicated  segment  shall  meet  all  of 
requirements in the following subparagraphs: (Amended on September 20, 2022) 

1. Debt securities shall be listed on the KOSPI market; 

2. Debt securities shall correspond to any of the following Items: 

(a)  In  the  case  of  green  bond,  it  shall  meet  the  Green  Bond  Guidelines  jointly 
established by the Financial Services Commission and the Ministry of Environment, 
the  Green  Bond  Principles  of  the  International  Capital  Market  Association,  the 
Climate Bonds Standard of the Climate Bonds Initiative, or other principles, etc. for 
socially responsible investment bonds recognized by the Exchange; 

(b)  In  the  case  of  social  bond,  it  shall  meet  the  Social  Bond  Principles  of  the 
International  Capital  Market  Association  or  other  principles,  etc.  for  socially 
responsible investment bonds recognized by the Exchange;   

(c) In the case of sustainability bond, it shall meet the Sustainability Bond Principles of 
the  International  Capital  Market  Association  or  other  principles,  etc.  for  socially 
responsible investment bonds recognized by the Exchange; and 

(d)  In  the  case  of  sustainability-linked  bonds,  it  shall  meet  the  Sustainability-Linked 
Bonds Principles of the International Capital Market Association or other principles, 
etc. for socially responsible investment bonds recognized by the Exchange. 

§5. Registration application 

(1)  If  a  debt  securities-listed  corporation  intends  to  register  debt  securities  in  the  dedicated 
segment,  it  shall  submit  to  the  Exchange  all  of  the  documents  in  each  of  the  following

subparagraphs: (Amended on November 23, 2021; September 20, 2022) 

1. Registration application in the Annex: Form No. 1; 

2. Socially responsible investment bond framework; 

3. Document falling under any of the following Items; 

(a) For use-of- proceeds bonds, documents stating the intended use of proceeds; 

(b) For Sustainability-linked bonds, investment prospectus (Regardless of the name of   

investment prospectus, investment contract, acquisition contract, etc., it shall refer to 
supplementary documents that are practically helpful in determining the financial or 
structural  characteristics  of  sustainability-linked  bonds  in  addition  to  the  socially 
responsible investment bond framework: the same shall apply hereinafter); 

4.  External  review  documents  that  fall  under  any  of  the  following  sub-items  issued  by 
external  reviewers  for  the  socially  responsible  investment  bonds  framework  of  a  debt 
securities-listed corporation (It may be substituted by a confirmation letter signed by the 
representative  director  of  the  concerned  debt  securities-listed  corporation  if  the 
Exchange deems it necessary).  In such  case, the  documents in the following sub-items 
are defined in accordance with the Guidelines for Social bonds External Reviews of the 
International Capital Market Association: 

(a) Second party opinion; 

(b) Verification; 

(c) Certification; and 

(d) Scoring/Rating. 

(2) Notwithstanding Paragraph 1, the submission of the relevant documents may be waived if 
the  Exchange  deems  it  not  necessary  in  consideration  of  the  use  of  proceeds  of  the  funds 
raised and the socially responsible investment bond framework . (Amended on September 20, 
2022) 

§6. Decision of registration 

(1) Upon the receipt of the application for  registration pursuant  to  [§5], the  Exchange shall 
decide without delay whether to register in the dedicated segment. However, if it is necessary 
to  correct  or  supplement  the  submitted  documents,  or  if  there  is  other  unavoidable 
circumstances, the registration decision shall be made when such circumstances are resolved. 
(Amended on September 20, 2022) 

(2) Upon the decision to  register pursuant to  Paragraph (1), the Exchange shall  classify  the

1. Green bond; 

2. Social bond;

§8. Ongoing registration requirements   
(Amended on November 23, 2021; September 20, 2022) 

(1)  A  debt  securities-listed  corporation  that  has  registered  socially  responsible  investment 
bonds  in  the  dedicated  segment  shall  submit  documents  in  each  of  the  following 
Subparagraphs to the Exchange: (Amended on November 23, 2021; September 20, 2022)

1.  In  the  case  where  registered  debt  securities  are  use-of-proceeds  bonds:  Documents  in 
each of the following Items. In such case, documents in Item (b) may be included in the 
contents of documents in Item (a); 

(a) Report on the use of proceeds that contains the detailed use of funds raised during the

(b) Impact report that states the expected or achieved effect. Performance report that   

contains details of annual key performance indicators 

    2.  In  the  case  where  registered  debt  securities  are  sustainability-linked  bonds: 
Performance  report  that  contains  the  performance  of  annual  key  performance 
indicators  and  the  measurements  of  sustainability  performance  targets  and  external 
review report that verifies the performance report. 

(2) A debt securities-listed corporation shall submit the report on the use of proceeds under 
Paragraph (1)1(a) in accordance with the following Items by the time the expenditure of the 
raised funds are completed. [September 20, 2022] 

1. Case where the report on the use of proceeds is submitted for the first time: The report 
on the use of proceeds shall be submitted by the end of the year following the year in 
which the issuance date  of the  relevant  use-of-proceeds  bonds falls.  Provided that,  in 
cases  where  the  concerned  use-of-proceeds  bonds  are  delisted  due  to  maturity, 
repayment before maturity, etc., the report on the use of proceeds shall be submitted by 
the date of delisting; and 

2. Case where the report on the use of proceeds is submitted after the submission under 
subparagraph  1:  The  report  on  the  use  of  proceeds  shall  be  submitted  by  the  end  of 
each  year  following  the  year  in  which  the  report  on  the  use  of  proceeds  is  submitted 
pursuant  to  Subparagraph  1.  Provided  that,  in  cases  where  the  concerned  use-of-
proceeds bonds are delisted due to maturity, repayment before maturity, etc., the report 
on the use of proceeds shall be submitted by the date of delisting. 

(3)  A  debt  securities-listed  corporation  shall  submit  the  impact  report  under  Paragraph 
(1)1(b) at least once from the time of completing the expenditure of the raised funds to the 
date of delisting. [September 20, 2022] 

(4) A debt securities-listed corporation shall submit the performance report and the external 
review report under Paragraph (1)2, including those of the  year in  which the final date of 
judgement (referring to the date on which whether the sustainability performance targets are 
achieved is judged regardless of the name of judgement date, observation date, etc.) for the 
sustainability  performance  targets  defined  in  the  socially  responsible  investment  bond 
framework  or  the  investment  prospectus,  in  accordance  with  each  of  the  following  Items. 
[September 20, 2022]

(6) Notwithstanding Paragraphs (1) and (2), the Exchange may waive the submission of the 
report on the use-of-proceeds bonds or set the submission deadline differently considering 
the time and details of the use of raised funds of the use-of-proceeds bonds. (Moved from 
Paragraph (3) on September 20, 2022) 

§9. Revocation of registration   

(1) In cases where the debt securities registered in the dedicated segment fall under any of the 
following  subparagraphs,  the  Exchange  shall  revoke  the  registration  of  their  dedicated 
segment: (Amended on November 23, 2021; September 20, 2022) 

1.  Case  where  the  debt  securities-listed  corporation  applies  for  the  withdrawal  of 

registration; 

2. Case where the debt securities concerned are delisted from the KOSPI market; 

3. Case where the use of raised funds of the use-of-proceeds bonds does not comply with 

the relevant standards. 

4. Case where a debt securities-listed corporation failed to submit either the report on the 
use  of  proceeds  under  [§8(1)1(a)]  and  the  impact  report  under  [§8(1)1(b)]  to  the 
Exchange within the submission deadline; 

5. Case where  a debt securities-listed corporation failed to submit either the performance 

6 

 
 
 
 
 
   
 
 
 
 
 
report  or  the  external  review  report  under  [§8(1)2]  to  the  Exchange  within  the 
submission deadline; and 

6.  Other  cases  where  the  Exchange  deems  it  necessary  to  revoke  registration  for  public 

interest and investor protection. 

(2) In cases where any event under each Subparagraph of Paragraph (1), the Exchange may 
disclose such event in the dedicated segment. [November 23, 2021] 

(3)  In  cases  where  a  debt  securities-listed  corporation  wishes  to  withdraw  its  registration 
pursuant  to  paragraph  (1)1,  it  shall  submit  an  application  in  [Annex:  Form  No.2]  to  the 
Exchange. [November 23, 2021] 

§10. Supplementary provisions 

Matters not specified in these Guidelines in relation to the operation of the dedicated segment 
shall be decided by the President of the KOSPI Market Division.
